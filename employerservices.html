LMO Application and Work Permits for Foreign Workers

A Labour Market Opinion (LMO) is a report issued by HRSDC (Human Resources and Skills Development Canada) that outlines the impact that hiring a foreign worker would have on Canada’s labour market and is the first step in recruiting those hard-to-fill positions in your company. Employers must to obtain this assessment before hiring a foreign worker.  Once a positive LMO has been issued, it must be forwarded to the employees abroad to enable them to obtain a visa and/or a work permit that will allow them to travel to Canada to start work. If the worker is already in Canada, a work permit renewal application can be submitted to allow the foreign national to remain in Canada.
